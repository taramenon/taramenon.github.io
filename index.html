building brains & experiences with technology, and vice versa.

A large part of my mission is to make a big difference for the world, whether being at the forefront or behind-the-scenes.
Although everything fascinates me, from history to English literature, I believe that technology will transform everything. A few fields of interest within AI: computer vision, autonomous driving, human-computer interfaces, neurotechnology, robotics, and NLP.

why neuroscience?

From a neuroscience perspective, cognition, vision, sensation, and perception are fascinating as well. I'm excited to see the future of virtual + augmented reality in conjunction with haptics, turning experiences into reality. I'd imagine it'd have a profound impact on society and relationships, so I gave a TED talk about it. Plus, computational neuro is super cool.

long-term #goals.

My primary interest lies in applying technology and bioinformatics to improve the field of medicine, healthcare, and patient care, particularly in underserved populations, underrepresented minorities and LGBTQ+ youth. I’m keeping up with venture capital (VC) and start-ups in an attempt to stay entrepreneurial.
